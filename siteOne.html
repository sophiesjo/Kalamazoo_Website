Some fun facts about Kalamazoo:


Kalamazoo is home to some famous small town businesses including:

  • Gibson Guitar Corporation
    • Orville Gibson became famous for the new variety of acoustic instruments he created.
  • Sweetwater's Donut Mill
    • Recognized by MSN City Guide as the "Best Donuts in America"
  • Alamo Drafthouse
    • This is a unique cinema with old fashioned habits and plenty of cold beer.


Did you know that Elvis Presely used to visit Kalamazoo?
In the 60s and 70s, Presely would come to Kalamazoo for the Gibson Guitar factory. He visited enough to the point that in the Columbia Plaza, suite 201 became known as his office.

Another notable figure that visited Kalamazoo is President Obama when awarding Western Michigan University with the first "Race to the Top Commencement Challenge" in 2010.

Here is a list of other famous figures who have been to Kalamazoo:

  • Derek Jeter and Greg Jennings
  • Ozzy Osbourne
  • Abraham Linkcoln
  • Glenn Miller
